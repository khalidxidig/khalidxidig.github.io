About Me

I am Khalid Mohamed Ali, a Civil Engineer & Architectural Designer focused on both architectural design and structural engineering.

I create 2D drawings , 3D models and renderings, interior visuals, and structural design support for residential and commercial projects. With a detail oriented and client focused approach, I turn ideas into clear, build ready drawings and realistic visuals.

My goal is to deliver high quality work on time and ensure every client is satisfied.

Skills & Tools

Architectural Design

Architectural planning and visualization for functional, modern spaces.

  • 2D Floor Plans, Elevations & Sections
  • Space Planning & Layout Design
  • Interior Design Visualization
  • Architectural Detailing

Structural Design

Structural layouts and coordination for safe, build-ready construction.

  • Structural Layout (Beams, Columns, Slabs, Foundations)
  • Basic Structural Analysis
  • Structural Detailing & Coordination

Software Skills

Industry tools for drafting, modeling, BIM, and realistic rendering.

  • SketchUp / 3ds Max (3D Modeling)
  • Lumion / V-Ray (Rendering & Visualization)

Other Skills

Practical support for project delivery, quantities, and reporting.

  • BOQ / Quantity Takeoff
  • Basic Cost Estimation
  • Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
